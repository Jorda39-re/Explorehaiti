Beautiful Places in Images

Citadelle Laferrière

Located in northern Haiti, it is one of the most important symbols of Haitian freedom and resilience, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Palais Sans-Souci

Located near Milot in northern Haiti, built between 1810 and 1813 under King Henri Christophe.

Bassin Bleu

Near Jacmel in southeastern Haiti, consists of three natural basins fed by waterfalls.

Grotte Marie-Jeanne

Located near Port-à-Piment, one of Haiti’s largest caves with underground passages and rock formations.

Jardin Botanique Des Cayes

Near Les Cayes, promotes biodiversity conservation and environmental education.

Labadee

A private beach destination on Haiti’s northern coast, offering beaches and sea excursions.
